IKEA's "Where Life Happens" advertising campaign, launched in 2017, aimed to connect with consumers by showcasing real-life moments in everyday settings, emphasizing the importance of home in people's lives. The campaign featured relatable scenarios, such as families gathering for meals, children playing, and couples enjoying quiet moments, highlighting how IKEA products facilitate these experiences. For instance, the ads included vignettes of diverse families in various locations, from urban apartments to suburban homes, illustrating the brand's versatility and appeal across different demographics. The campaign also utilized social media platforms, encouraging customers to share their own "life happens" moments using the hashtag #WhereLifeHappens, fostering community engagement. Overall, the strategy focused on emotional storytelling to reinforce IKEA's position as a brand that understands and enhances the everyday lives of its customers.
