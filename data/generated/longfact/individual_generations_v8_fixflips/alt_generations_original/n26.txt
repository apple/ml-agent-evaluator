Norton Antivirus is a cybersecurity software developed by NortonLifeLock, originally created by Peter Norton in 1991. It provides real-time protection against malware, viruses, spyware, and other online threats, utilizing advanced technologies like machine learning and behavioral analysis. The software has evolved over the years, with significant updates such as Norton 360, which includes features like a VPN, password manager, and cloud backup, launched in 2019. Norton Antivirus has been recognized for its effectiveness, receiving various awards, including the AV-TEST Best Protection award multiple times. As of October 2023, Norton continues to be a prominent player in the cybersecurity market, serving millions of users worldwide.
