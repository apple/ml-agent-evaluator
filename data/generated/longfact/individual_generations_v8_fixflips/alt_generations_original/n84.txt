Cloud9 is a prominent eSports organization based in Los Angeles, California, founded in 2013 by Jack and Paullie. The organization has fielded teams in various games, including League of Legends, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, and Valorant, achieving significant success; for instance, their League of Legends team won the North American League of Legends Championship Series (NALCS) in the Summer Split of 2013. Notable players who have been part of Cloud9 include Tyler "Tyler1" Steinkamp, who gained fame in the League of Legends community, and Shahzeeb "ShahZaM" Khan, a key player in their Valorant roster. Cloud9 has also made headlines for their performance at major events, such as their third-place finish at the 2016 World Championship in League of Legends. As of 2023, Cloud9 continues to be a major player in the eSports scene, with a diverse roster and a strong fanbase.
