The Eaton Cutler-Hammer C30CNE is a lighting contactor designed for controlling lighting loads in commercial and industrial applications. It features a 30-amp rating and operates on a 120V coil, making it suitable for various lighting systems, including fluorescent and LED fixtures. The contactor is equipped with a robust design that ensures durability and reliability, often used in settings such as office buildings and warehouses. For example, in a project completed in 2022 at a manufacturing facility in Chicago, Illinois, the C30CNE was installed to manage the facility's extensive lighting system, enhancing energy efficiency. Additionally, the contactor's compatibility with automation systems allows for integration with smart building technologies, providing users with advanced control options.
