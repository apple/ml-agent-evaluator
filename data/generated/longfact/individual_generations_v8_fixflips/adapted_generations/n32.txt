The Eaton Cutler-Hammer C30CNE is a lighting contactor designed for controlling lighting loads in commercial and industrial electrical systems. It features a 30-amp rating and operates at 600 volts, making it suitable for various applications, including parking lots and large facilities. The contactor is equipped with a coil voltage of 120V AC, allowing it to be easily integrated into standard control circuits. For example, it can be used in conjunction with a time clock or occupancy sensor to automate lighting control, enhancing energy efficiency. Eaton Power Electronics Inc., a global leader in power management, has been providing innovative electrical solutions since its founding in 1911, with headquarters located in Dublin, Ireland.
