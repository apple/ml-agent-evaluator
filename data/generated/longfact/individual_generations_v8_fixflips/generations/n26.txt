Norton Antivirus is a cybersecurity software developed by NortonLifeLock, originally created by Peter Norton in 1991. It provides real-time protection against malware, viruses, spyware, and other online threats, utilizing advanced technologies such as machine learning and behavioral analysis. The software has evolved over the years, with significant updates like Norton 360, which includes features such as a VPN, password manager, and cloud backup, released in 2019. Norton Antivirus has been recognized for its effectiveness, receiving accolades from independent testing labs like AV-Test and AV-Comparatives. As of October 2023, Norton continues to be a popular choice for both individual users and businesses, with millions of subscribers worldwide.
