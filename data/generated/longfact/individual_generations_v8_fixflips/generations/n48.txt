OpenAI is an artificial intelligence research organization founded in December 2015 by Elon Musk, Sam Altman, Greg Brockman, Ilya Sutskever, Wojciech Zaremba, and John Schulman, among others. Based in San Francisco, California, OpenAI aims to ensure that artificial general intelligence (AGI) benefits all of humanity. In June 2020, OpenAI released the language model GPT-3, which gained widespread attention for its ability to generate human-like text and was made available through an API. In November 2022, OpenAI launched ChatGPT, a conversational AI model that quickly became popular for its interactive capabilities. As of 2023, OpenAI has continued to advance its research and applications, including the release of GPT-4, further enhancing its impact on various industries.
